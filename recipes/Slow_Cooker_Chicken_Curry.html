Slow Cooker Chicken Curry


This is my favorite meal to prep for the week. It's made from relatively cheap
ingredients and easy to package out into separate lunches. Serve it with rice or bread!

I created this recipe a while back by combining a few similar recipes that I had found on google. I had just started trying to save money and needed to lower my takeout bill by at least 75%. It makes enough food for about seven moderately sized meals. Serving it with rice or bread makes it last longer. The great thing about it is, you can really add whatever vegetables you like, and you can also spice it up to taste. Real chefs probably scoff at the idea of using a Croc Pot, but for the average person who works and tries to study in there free time, the Croc Pot has become an invaluable tool for to save money and make delicious meals. I hope you enjoy this recipe. I have made it countless times and I hardly ever get sick of it. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Whole Fat Yogurt (not greek yogurt)
